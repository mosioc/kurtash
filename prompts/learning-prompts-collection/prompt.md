# Learning Collection

## 1. Learn Anything Faster With the 80/20 Principle

💬 **Prompt:**
> _“I want to learn about _**_\[insert topic]_**_. Identify and share the most important 20% of learnings from this topic that will help me understand 80% of it.”_

## 2. Develop Any New Skill From Scratch (Beginner Plan)

💬 **Prompt:**
> _“I want to learn / get better at _**_\[insert skill]_**_. I am a complete beginner. Create a 30-day learning plan that will help a beginner like me learn and improve this skill.”_

## 3. Build Expertise With Consistent Learning

💬 **Prompt:**
> _“I want to learn _**_\[insert skill]_**_. Create a 30-day learning plan that will help a beginner like me learn and improve this skill.”_

## 4. Summarize Long Books, Articles, or PDFs in Minutes

💬 **Prompt:**
> _“Summarize the text below and give me a bullet-point list with key insights and the most important facts.”_**_\[insert text]_**

## 5. Train ChatGPT to Generate the Best Prompts for You

💬 **Prompt:**
> _“You are an AI designed to help _**_\[insert profession]_**_. Generate a list of the 10 best prompts for yourself. The prompts should be about _**_\[insert topic]_**_.”_

## 6. Master Any Skill in Two Months

💬 **Prompt:**
> _“I have 3 free days a week and 2 months. Design a crash study plan to master _**_\[insert skill]_**_.”_

## 7. Create a Resume That Stands Out From the Crowd

💬 **Prompt:**
> _“Analyze my professional details below and create a persuasive resume that will help me get the job of _**_\[insert role/company]_**_.”_

## 8. Prepare for an Interview Like a Top Candidate

💬 **Prompt:**
> _“Act as a world-class interviewer. Ask questions about _**_\[enter topic]_**_. I will answer these questions. After I answer, suggest the best possible answer and provide another question.”_
